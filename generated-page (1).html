Travel Protection

Safeguard your journey against the unexpected.

Travel protection plans by Generali offer valuable coverage to safeguard your trip and provide assistance in case of unexpected events.

Coverage Benefits

Comprehensive protection for every step of your journey.

Cancellation & Interruption

Covers non-refundable expenses if you cancel or cut your trip short due to covered reasons.

Emergency Medical

Pays for treatment if you fall ill or are injured, especially internationally where regular insurance may not apply.

Lost or Delayed Bag

Report missing bags to the airline for a file number. BagAssist will track location and status until returned.

Travel Delay Expenses

Reimbursement for food, accommodation, and transport if stuck due to weather, airline issues, or other delays.

Emergency Assistance

Find local doctors, get cash transfers (up to $500), legal connections, and return help for companions.

24/7 Assistance Services

Access to emergency hotlines for travel, legal, or medical support anytime, anywhere in the world.
